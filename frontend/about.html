About the Club

Empowering students through technology since 2020.

Our Mission

MPSC IT CLUB aims to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world technology application. We provide a platform for students to explore programming, robotics, graphic design, and more.

Our Vision

To create a community of innovators who will lead the digital future of Bangladesh. We believe in "Innovation for All" and work towards making technology accessible and fun for every student.
